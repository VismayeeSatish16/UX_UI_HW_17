A little bit about Myself

My name is Vismayee Satish and I'm a UI/UX designer who takes pride in crafting visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that enhance productivity. My primary focus is collaborating with clients to develop innovative and impactful UI designs that maximize their product's potential.

In addition to my design work, I have over three years of experience counselling and providing yoga therapy to children with special needs at the Vydehi Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre in Bangalore, India. For the past six years, I have also been freelancing as an online yoga instructor and counsellor. Furthermore, I have been serving as an educator in Kindergarten services for the past three years in Australia.

My Values as a UI/UX Designer

As a UI/UX designer and a counsellor, my core values revolve around creating designs that are not only visually appealing but also enhance the user experience. I believe in using a user-centered approach to design that focuses on the needs and goals of the end user. As a UI/UX designer, I value simplicity and clarity in design. I strive to create designs that are intuitive, easy to navigate, and aesthetically pleasing. I also believe in the importance of usability testing and iterative design to create products that truly meet the needs of users. As a psychologist, I value empathy and understanding in design. I believe that a deep understanding of human behavior and emotions is essential to creating effective and meaningful designs. I also believe in designing with inclusivity in mind, considering the diverse needs and perspectives of different user groups. Overall, my values as a UI/UX designer and psychologist intersect in the belief that design should be functional, aesthetically pleasing, and emotionally resonant. I strive to create designs that not only meet the needs of users but also enhance their lives in meaningful ways.
